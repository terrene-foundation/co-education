# /explain $ARGUMENTS

Explain a concept clearly, building from intuition to formal understanding.

## Protocol

1. **Assess the student's current level** — what do they already know?
2. **Start with intuition** — analogy or real-world example
3. **Build to formal explanation** — definitions, relationships, mechanisms
4. **Show worked examples** — concrete application of the concept
5. **Connect to the assignment** — how does this concept matter for their current work?
6. **Check understanding** — ask a question that tests whether they got it

## Three Levels

- **Beginner**: Analogy + plain language. "Think of it like..."
- **Intermediate**: Formal definitions + worked example. "The technical definition is..."
- **Advanced**: Edge cases + connections to other concepts. "This breaks down when..."

Match the student's level. If they say they don't understand, drop a level and use a different analogy.
